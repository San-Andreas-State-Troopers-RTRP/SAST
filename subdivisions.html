SAST Subdivisions

Highway Patrol: Enforce traffic laws and patrol highways across the state.

Air Support Unit: Operates helicopters and drones to assist in pursuits and searches.

K-9 Unit: Utilizes trained dogs for drug detection, searches, and suspect apprehension.

Criminal Investigations: Handles complex investigations, case files, and forensic analysis.

Motor Unit: Motorcycle officers for quick response and escort duties.
