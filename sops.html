Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

  • All troopers must wear proper uniforms and carry approved gear.
  • Radio discipline is required at all times.
  • Pursuits must be called out clearly and approved when necessary.
  • Use of force must always follow the Use of Force Continuum.
  • Scene command goes to the highest-ranking on-scene officer.
  • Professionalism and RP realism must be maintained at all times.
