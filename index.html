About SAST

The San Andreas State Troopers (SAST) is a premier law enforcement agency dedicated to maintaining law and order across the state. From high-speed pursuits to assisting local departments, our troopers are trained to handle it all with professionalism and precision.

Departments & Divisions

Patrol Division

First responders to emergencies and routine calls across highways and interstates.

Air Support Unit

Provides aerial surveillance and tactical support during operations and pursuits.

Criminal Investigations

Investigates major crimes and works closely with other law enforcement entities.
